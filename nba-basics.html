NBA Basic Rules

  • Each games is 48 minutes long divided into 4 quarters of 12 minute lengths.
  • There are three ways to get points: 3 pointer, 2 pointer, or free throw.
  • Each team has 5 starters on the floor with a bench of 8 players.
  • In order to move, you must dribble the ball.
  • Once the basketball is inbounded, you have 8 seconds to cross the half-court line.
  • Each time you possess the ball, you have 24 seconds to shoot it; if not, the other team gets awarded possession of the ball.
